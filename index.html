Welcome to Tom's Antique Shop!

In the fall of 1993, Tom Anderson's passion for collecting treasures from all over the world finally combined with his wife Linda's enterprenurial spirit (and her desire reclaim their spare bedrooms) and Tom's Antique Shop was born. Typewriters

An antique typewriter is a great way to bring warmth and nostalgia to a study. We have collected over 30 unique pieces from all over the Usited States from various time periods, and we're sure to have just what you're looking for.

Compasses

Small or large, a compass is a beautiful and versatile piece. Put it on a long chain to create a beautiful and functional necklace, or mount it on the wall to bring a nautical theme to a study or den full of dark wood.

Antique Furniture

Furniture today is just not made the way it used to be. Real hardwoods, rich fabrics, and painstaking attention to detail are the hallmarks of antique furniture pieces and they're a great addition to any room in your home.
